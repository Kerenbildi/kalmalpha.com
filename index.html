Does it really work while I sleep?

The science of subconscious reprogramming shows that the hypnagogic state, the threshold between waking and sleep, is when your brain is most receptive to new patterns. KALM is built for both ends of that window: falling asleep and waking up.

Why does my own voice matter?

Research in psychology and neuroscience consistently shows that hearing your own voice triggers different neural pathways than hearing a stranger's. When you record yourself saying your affirmations, the brain processes them as memory, not instruction. That is the difference.

What is Schumann resonance and will I hear it?

7.83Hz is the electromagnetic resonance of the Earth's atmosphere, the frequency the planet itself vibrates at. It corresponds closely to human alpha and theta brainwave states associated with deep relaxation, creativity, and receptivity. You will not hear it directly because 7.83Hz falls below the threshold of human hearing, which begins around 20Hz. The body responds to it through electromagnetic entrainment, not through the ears. This is why KALM plays calm ambient music alongside it: your ears have something to rest on, your nervous system settles, and the frequency does its work underneath. Both are running simultaneously throughout your session.

Is this a subscription?

Which languages are supported?

English, French, German, Spanish, and Hebrew. The AI generates your affirmation script in whichever language you choose, including languages where the syntax and emotional register differ significantly from English.
